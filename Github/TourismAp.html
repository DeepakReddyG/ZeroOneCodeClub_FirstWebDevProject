About Andhra Pradesh Tourism

Andhra Pradesh, a southeastern state in India, is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, vibrant festivals, and diverse natural landscapes. From the serene beaches of Visakhapatnam to the majestic hills of Araku Valley, Andhra Pradesh offers a wide range of attractions for tourists.

Popular Places to Visit

  • Visakhapatnam Beaches
  • Tirupati Balaji Temple
  • Araku Valley
  • Konaseema Delta
  • Krishna River
