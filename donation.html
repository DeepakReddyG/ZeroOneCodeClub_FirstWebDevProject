What do donations pay for?

The largest cost we have is the Blackboard Collaborate online classroom annual subscription at just under $5,000 a year. Under this subscription, each teaching volunteer has a classroom to use for the school year.

Our second largest cost is the server that our site/LMS runs on. We serve about 7,000 students each year, so it takes a fully dedicated server with only the project on it so there are enough resources to meet peak demand and hold all of our data. Our introductory price wil expire, so I estimate the cost this coming school year will be about $2600.
