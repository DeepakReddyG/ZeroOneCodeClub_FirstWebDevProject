About India Tourism

The top 5 tourist spots in India include Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage site located in Agra, India, renowned for its exquisite white marble mausoleum built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.The Red Fort, located in Delhi, India, is an iconic historical fortress and UNESCO World Heritage Site built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century.The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is a sacred Sikh shrine adorned in gold, located in Amritsar, India.Jaipur, the Pink City of India, mesmerizes with its vibrant culture, majestic forts, and bustling bazaars. Pondicherry, a charming coastal town in southern India known for its French colonial architecture and vibrant cultural heritage.

Popular Places to Visit
